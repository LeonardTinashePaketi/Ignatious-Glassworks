Common Questions

Frequently asked

Yes — our spigot-mounted and channel-base systems are fully top-rail-free. The glass panels are structurally self-supporting and comply with SANS 10400 safety requirements. This gives you an uninterrupted view with zero visual obstruction at the top.
Balustrades are typically installed with 10mm or 12mm toughened safety glass depending on the span and load requirements. Shower enclosures use 8mm or 10mm low-iron clear glass. All glass is certified toughened or laminated safety glass.
All our structural glass installations comply with SANS 10400 (National Building Regulations) for glass in buildings. We use SABS-certified toughened safety glass throughout and can provide certification documentation upon request.
Both mounting options are available. Side-fix (fascia mount) and top-fix (core-drilled or surface-mounted spigots) are standard in our product range. The choice depends on your slab structure, aesthetic preference, and available fixing depth.
We offer Chrome (polished stainless), Matte Black (powder-coated), Brushed Gold, and Brushed Stainless Steel. Hardware is matched across clamps, hinges, handles, and rail brackets for a cohesive look. Custom powder-coat colours can be arranged on request.
A standard residential installation (balustrade or shower) typically takes 1–2 days once glass is fabricated. Larger projects with multiple systems are usually completed within a week. Lead time from approved quote to installation is typically 2–3 weeks.
